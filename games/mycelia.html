Mycelia

A farming game set on the island of Mycelia. Explore the island and uncover the secrets of agriculture. Cultivate the unique environment and expand your farm to the undiscovered parts of the island. Sell your crops and goods at the seaside market to make a profit.



Explore

Step into the enchanting world of Mycelia, a whimsical pixel-art farming game that invites you to embark on a journey of discovery. As you arrive on the island of Mycelia, you'll be greeted by its lush landscapes, mysterious forests, and hidden nooks. Delve into the lore and history of Mycelia to uncover the forgotten practices that will aid you in your farming endeavors.



Cultivate

Embrace the role of a budding farmer as you dive into the heart of Mycelia's agricultural essence. Learn to adapt to the island's ever-changing weather patterns and cultivate a variety of crops that thrive in Mycelia's unique ecosystems. Discover rare seeds that, when cared for with precision and love, result in extraordinary plants with mystical properties. As you tend to your fields, you'll unlock the secrets of crop rotation, soil enrichment, and the delicate balance between nature and cultivation.



Expand

As your farming expertise grows, so too does your domain. Gradually unlock the ability to extend your farm's borders beyond its initial boundaries. Traverse into uncharted territories, each with its own challenges and opportunities. Clear overgrown paths, construct bridges over bubbling streams, and restore crumbling structures to create new spaces for cultivation. Establish outposts on the outer islands, allowing you to diversify your farming operations and take advantage of unique microclimates.
